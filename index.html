About Us

We are a family-oriented, Xbox One gaming clan with a very colorful vocabulary and a complete disregard for social decency. Although our main game of choice is Call of Duty: WWII, we dabble in various other game types from First-Person Shooters, to RPGs, to sporting games.

TNFM was founded during the 2017 Holiday season, starting off as a group chat on Facebook and progressing quickly into the clan you see today.
